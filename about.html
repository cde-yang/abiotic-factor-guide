About This Site

🧬 What Is Abiotic Factor Guide?

Abiotic Factor Guide is a fan-made, player-written strategy wiki dedicated to Abiotic Factor, the survival horror game developed by Deep Field Games. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Deep Field Games in any way.

Every guide on this site is written by players who have spent hundreds of hours exploring, dying, and mastering the GATE Cascade Research Facility. We test every strategy, verify every spawn location, and update our content as the game evolves through Early Access and beyond.

📝 Our Content Philosophy

  • Specific, not generic. We don't write "dodge the boss attacks." We write "when the boss raises its right arm for 0.6 seconds, dodge backward."
  • Tested, not theorycrafted. Every stat, damage number, and strategy is verified in-game before publication.
  • Updated regularly. Abiotic Factor is in active development. We update guides as patches drop.
  • No filler, no fluff. We respect your time. Every paragraph has actionable information.

📬 Contact / Contribute

Found an error? Want to contribute a guide? Have a strategy we haven't covered? We welcome community contributions.

This site is maintained as a free resource for the Abiotic Factor community. We use Google AdSense to cover hosting costs — that's it. No paywalls, no premium guides, no nonsense.
